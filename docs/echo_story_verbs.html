Notes:

- Some verbs appear in the text only in one aspect

- Motion verbs often have multiple prefixed forms with different meanings (бежать, побежать, подбежать, добежать, разбежаться)

- "Использовать" has identical imperfective and perfective forms in modern usage

- "Походить" in the sense of "to resemble" is imperfective only

- Several verbs are repeated as they appear in both the story and instructional sections
