Notes:

- Some verbs appear in the text only in one aspect

- Motion verbs often have multiple prefixed forms with different meanings

- "Давай" is the imperative form of "давать" meaning "let's (do something)"

- "Позвякивать" is an iterative/frequentative form meaning repeated light jingling sounds

- Several verbs appear multiple times in different contexts throughout the story
