Russian translation project

I was recently tipped off by a fellow user of the SDF Public Access UNIX System about a project to translate a Soviet children's book about physics, "Fizika dlya malyshey" or Physics for Kids by L. L. Sikoruk (published by Izdatelstvo Pedagogika in 1983), into English.
